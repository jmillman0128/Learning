Newton's Three Laws of Motion

  1. First Law (Law of Inertia): An object at rest stays at rest and an object in motion stays in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced force.
  2. Second Law (Law of Acceleration): The acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the net force acting upon it and inversely proportional to its mass (F = ma).
  3. Third Law (Action and Reaction): For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
